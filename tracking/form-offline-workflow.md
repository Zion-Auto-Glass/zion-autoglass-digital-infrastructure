# Lead Form Offline Conversion Workflow

This document describes how lead form submissions are reviewed, qualified, and uploaded as offline conversions.

## Form Sources
- Website lead submission forms
- Campaign-linked form traffic from Google Ads

## Operational Status
- Lead forms were temporarily paused
- Forms are expected to be re-enabled
- Workflow remains active and documented

## Qualification Criteria
Lead form submissions are reviewed based on:
- Valid contact information
- Service relevance
- Geographic eligibility
- Exclusion of spam or automated submissions

## Review Process
1. Submissions are reviewed outside of this repository
2. Leads are classified as qualified or unqualified
3. Only qualified leads proceed to offline upload

## Offline Upload
- Qualified form leads are uploaded to Google Ads
- Matching is performed using allowed identifiers
- Uploads are aligned to original ad interactions

## Exclusions
- Spam submissions
- Test entries
- Incomplete or invalid forms
- Submissions lacking service intent

## Data Handling
- Raw form data remains offline
- No customer information is stored in this repository
