# Tracking Glossary

This glossary defines key terms used in Zion Auto Glass tracking and conversion workflows.

## Conversion
A user action that represents potential or realized business value, including qualified phone calls and qualified lead form submissions.

## Online Conversion
A conversion recorded directly by Google Ads or associated tags at the time of user interaction.

## Offline Conversion
A conversion uploaded to Google Ads after review and qualification, matched to an original ad interaction.

## Qualified Call
A phone call that meets duration and intent criteria indicating legitimate service interest.

## Qualified Lead
A lead form submission that contains valid information and service-related intent.

## Unqualified Interaction
Any call or form submission that is spam, test, vendor-related, fraudulent, or otherwise not indicative of customer intent.

## Call-Only Campaign
A Google Ads campaign designed to drive phone calls directly from ads.

## Lead Form Campaign
A Google Ads campaign designed to drive web-based form submissions.

## Identifier
A value used to match offline conversions to ad interactions, such as permitted click or call identifiers.

## Excluded Data
Sensitive or raw data (phone numbers, exports, billing records) that is intentionally kept outside this repository.
